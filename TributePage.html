Dave Asprey

Founder of Bulletproof

Here is an overview of Dave :

  • An entrepreneur, businessman and author from Albuquerque, NM
  • Founded Bulletproof 360, Inc. in 2013 and Bulletproof Nutrition Inc. in 2014
  • Creator of Bulletproof Coffee and the Bulletproof diet
  • Known for being early to exploit the Internet for commerce(1994)
  • Held executive and director positions for companies including Trend Micro, Blue Coat Systems and Citrix Systems
  • Graduated from University of California, Santa Barbara
  • Created one of the first working instances of cloud computing
  • Was the vice president of cloud security for Trend Micro before he left to run his own business full-time
  • Initially started the Bulletproof brand after developing Bulletproof Coffee
  • Developed low-mold coffee beans, oils and supplements and started selling them on his website in 2011
  • Runs a podcast, "Bulletproof Radio," which was downloaded 10.5 million times in 2014
  • In 2014, Asprey authored The Bulletproof Diet published by Rodale Books
  • On July 25, 2015, Asprey opened a cafe in Santa Monica selling Bulletproof Coffee and high fat foods
  • In July, 2015, Asprey raised $9 million from Trinity Ventures to expand the company
